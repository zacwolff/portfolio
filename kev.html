CHALLENGE

Design, develop, and ship the easiest, no-nonsense light meter for iPhone.

Being a hobbyist filmmaker, I needed a dead-simple way to measure accurate white balance and that is how KEV, short for Kelvin (the unit for colour temperature) was created. With the help of my remote-friend of five years, Satnamand I went to the drawing board and conceptualised this app.

About Kev

Photographers and filmmakers know that setting and correcting white balance is essential to producing expected pleasing visuals when shooting photos or video. Most cameras including mobile phones have a built in Auto White Balance feature which lets the camera set what it thinks is the best temperature and then adjust that setting.

However, this does not always work as expected. Several factors could lead to bad footage when you let your camera set its own white balance. One of such factors is when weather conditions which can change dramatically from warm to cooler temperatures almost rapidly sometimes and this can lead to inconsistent visuals as cameras will constantly try to adjust these settings during a shoot
